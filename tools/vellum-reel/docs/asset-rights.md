# Asset and rights guide

VellumReel is a production tool. It does not grant rights to books, cover art, voices, music, images or generated media.

Before publishing a video:

- use text you wrote, public-domain text, licensed excerpts, or content allowed by applicable law;
- use your own voice or a voice for which you have explicit permission;
- verify the commercial-use terms of every TTS, image and music provider;
- keep a provenance record for prompts, source files, licenses and generation settings;
- follow platform rules for labeling AI-generated or synthetic media;
- do not commit signed asset URLs or provider project identifiers.

The public demos use fictional text and CSS-rendered fallback scenes so the repository can be cloned without media rights ambiguity.
